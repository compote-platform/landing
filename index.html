About us

Increase sales by making others compete for your products

The Solution

We have the solution for you, which basically converts a promotion campaign to a sales campaign.
Our platform enables you to promote your business by selling products through engaging gamified contests.

How it Works?

Step 1

You pick products

Create a campaign with your goods and services to sell.
All we need is a description, a promo image and stock info - as easy as that.

Step 2

People compete for it

Let the Compote start! We will create contests with your products as the grand prizes. Each contest is a guaranteed sale, every contestant is your potential customer.

Step 3

Your sales & audience grow

Once the contest is finished - you have a deal. Deliver prizes to winners and don't stop just there! As a bonus, you can provide consolation prizes & special offers for the non-winners to boost your audience.
